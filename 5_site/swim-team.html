View Ridge Swim Team

The View Ridge Swim Team is open to all children ages 5 to high school graduation who are members of the club. Team goals are hard work, friendship, sportmanship, and fun. All abilities and levels of interest are welcome. Last summer over 200 View Ridge swimmers participated.

View Ridge is a member of the Seattle Summer Swim League which is comprised of 16 teams divided evenly into North and South Divisions. The SSSL has been in existence since 1960 to promote recreational and competitive swimming.
