A Little Bit of Urban Paradise

About the Club

V iew Ridge Swim & Tennis Club was established in 1958 as a facility where families could gather to enjoy swimming, friendships, and other activities. Presently there are 420 families. We are open from the middle of May until the middle of September. We have a varied schedule offering a Swim Team, Lessons, Open Swim, Adult Lap Swimming, an Adult Swim Team, and many social activities.

Club Activities

T he club offers a world class swimming pool, top-of-the-line tennis courts, and breathtaking views of the trees behind the water slide. In addition to team activities, the Club offers both group and private Swim Lessons and Tennis Lessons.
