Class Levels

Minnow I and II (for ages 4 - 5)

These classes recognize the developmental limitations of children this age. The are designed to move the young swimmer from comfort and enjoyment of the basic strokes. Patience and fun are emphasized.

Minnow III (for ages 4 - 5)

These classes recognize the developmental limitations of children this age. The are designed to move the young swimmer from comfort and enjoyment of the basic strokes. Patience and fun are emphasized.

Non-Floater

This class is for older children (5 & up) who need to become comfortable in the water. They will work toward putting the face in the water and ultimately floating. These children usually have some real fear so patience will be stressed.

Floater

For older children (5 & up) who are somewhat comfortable in the water, can put the face in for a period of time, and are floating well. We will work on many prerequisites to swimming.

Beginner I

These classes recognize the developmental limitations of children this age. The are designed to move the young swimmer from comfort and enjoyment of the basic strokes. Patience and fun are emphasized.

Beginner II

This class is for the little ones who are very comfortable in the water and can easily float anywhere in the pool. Because of their age they have difficulties with the coordination of traditional strokes.

Advanced Beginner

This class is for the little ones who are very comfortable in the water and can easily float anywhere in the pool. Because of their age they have difficulties with the coordination of traditional strokes.

Racing

This class is for the little ones who are very comfortable in the water and can easily float anywhere in the pool. Because of their age they have difficulties with the coordination of traditional strokes.
