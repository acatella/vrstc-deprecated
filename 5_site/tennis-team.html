View Ridge Tennis Team

The Youth Tennis Team is open to all VRSTC members currently enrolled in K-12. The team is divided into Elementary (K-5), Middle School (6-8), and High School (9-12). Which team you are on is determined by the grade you will be in during the Fall of 2016. Some players will be allowed to play up (ES to MS, MS to HS) depending on ability as determined by the coaches.
