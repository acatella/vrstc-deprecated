Club Events

Kid Parties Adult Parties Family Events Club Events

Kid Parties

Pre-School

Go Bananas with Curious George! If you are 3 to 5 years old, join us in the clubhouse for food, fun and games with Curious George. ($5)

Elementary

Reptile Madness: for kids entering kindergarten through those who just finished 5th grade. The Reptile Man will be coming to the pool with reptiles for people see and touch.  There will also be food and reptile crafts. 

Middle School

Get your groove on under the disco ball at the VRSTC clubhouse! Pizza, drinks, and dessert will be served as you hang out with VRSTC friends and dance the night away.

High School

Enjoy the pool with just your high school friends or play games and each food up in the clubhouse.  The pool and surrounding area is yours. 

Adult Parties

The Adult Party

Be sure to clear your Friday night for this annual event. Enjoy a beautiful Seattle evening dining and drinking by the pool, enjoying fellow member’s company, and belting out your best karaoke.

Wine & Cheese

Bring your favorite bottle of wine to share stories and cheer with your friends – old and new on the upper deck of VRSTC. Cheese will be provided.

Family Events

Back to Summer BBQ

All members are invited to enjoy a BBQ View Ridge style.  All food and drink is provided.  Bring a dessert to share, a healthy appetite, and your family to our annual Back to Summer BBQ.

Crafternoons

Feeling creative while soaking up some sunshine and swimming in the pool? Then crafternoons are for YOU! Check the pool calendar and stop by to explore your artistic side.

Game Nights

Spend the evening with friends playing your favorite board game and eating brownies.  Bring a board game and or playing cars from home to share with others.

Food Truck Fridays

Enjoy dinner at the pool with the tasty food from a local food truck.

Fourth of July

Gone are the days of walking out on the greased pole jetting out over the high dive, but there’s still plenty of fun to be had by all! Money dive, sack races, tug-o-war, candy hunt, three-legged race, watermelon eating… lots of old fashioned fun!

Club Events

Water Show

A VRSTC tradition! The Water Show! Members ages 5 – 18 (must be able to swim) are invited to participate in the Water Show. All participants must be able to attend practices leading up to the big night. Super fun theme, costumes, deck and pool routines make for a very entertaining night at the pool.

VRSTC Overnight

All members ages 8 – 12 are invited to bring a tent, sleeping bag and their favorite teddy bear to this beloved annual event at the pool for a night of hanging out, swimming, and sleeping under the stars. Sign up in the office. Opportunities for parents to chaperone. No guests.

Hours

Monday - Friday: 8am - 10pm
Saturday & Sunday: 6am - 11pm
