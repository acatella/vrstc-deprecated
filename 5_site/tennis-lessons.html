Class Levels

10 & Under Tennis (5-10 Year Olds)

We will be offering the USTA 10 and Under Tennis format for the (10 and under) Beginner and Intermediate classes. 10 and Under Tennis incorporates a format using age appropriate tennis equipment and tailors the size of the court so that kids can learn, rally, play and compete in a way that is more FUN.

Red Ball (Ages 5-7)

Groups span from beginner to intermediate levels. Strong focus on development of eye hand coordination, sending and receiving the ball, sportsmanship and fun. Players will be evaluated on the first day and then divided into compatible groups by skill.

Orange Ball (Ages 8-10)

Groups span from beginner to intermediate levels. Strong focus on development of eye hand coordination, sending and receiving the ball, sportsmanship and fun. Players will be evaluated on the first day and then divided into compatible groups by skill. The intermediate class will begin at 1 PM. All classes will run for 30 minutes.

Junior Beginner (Ages 11-3 & 14-17)

This program focuses heavily on solid stroke development, strategy and footwork patterns.

Junior Intermediate (Ages 11-13 & 14-17)

This program will teach players to rally with more consistent depth and placement and will focus on developing a first and second serve. Players will build on fundamental strokes and techniques. They will be introduced to match play strategies and effective footwork.
