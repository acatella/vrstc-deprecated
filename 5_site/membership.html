Membership

Membership Information

  • We are a member owned club, limited by our by-laws to 480 family memberships. There are presently around 470 members.
  • Membership boundaries - North: N. 110th Street, South: Ship Canal, East: Lake Washington, West: 25th N.E.
  • We are open mid-May to mid-September. The tennis courts are open all year.
  • The membership fee is presently $5000 (All but $1,700 of this will be refunded with the sale of the membership.)
  • Annual dues are $800.
  • There is an additional $500 yearly "capital dues" payment for the recent remodel. 50% of this will be refunded with the sale of the membersip.
  • There is presently a waiting list of around 1,000. The number of new members varies from year to year, so it is impossible to predict how long it might take to become a member. The club is always interested in bringing in new members - new members add to the vitality of the club. However, we are restricted by capacity, bylaws, and article of incorporation.
  • We charge a one-time fee of $100 to be on the waiting list.
